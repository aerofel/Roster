Before you continue
Please take a moment to read and acknowledge the following.
🔒
Your data stays with you

This application runs entirely in your browser. No data is ever transmitted, intercepted, collected, or stored on any external server. Your schedule feed is fetched directly by your browser and never leaves your device. You are welcome to inspect the source code of this page at any time to verify this.

🔍
What this application does

This is a personal schedule viewer — a display layer that fetches your crew roster from your existing ICS calendar feed and presents it in an enhanced, more readable format. It does not replace, modify, or interfere with your official scheduling system in any way.

⚠️
Accuracy & responsibility

Schedule information displayed here is parsed from your calendar feed and may contain errors, omissions, or display inaccuracies due to parsing limitations or software bugs. Always consult your official scheduling system as the authoritative source for duty times, flights, and assignments. The developer accepts no responsibility for any decisions made based on information displayed in this application.
